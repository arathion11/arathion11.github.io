My Trip To Alaska

Over the summer, I went on a cruise to Alaska with my family. We went on various excursions and got to see a lot of the beautiful scenery. It was an incredible experience that I want to share with you guys.

On my excursions I saw orcas, glaciers, large valleys, and even boarded an open train.

The ship had the most amazing food- I wrote down all the dishes I ate to both remember them and hopefully encourage you guys to try some out.

I would love to get to know you guys and keep in touch!

If you want to book your own cruise, I have provided the links and information to Celebrity Cruises, which is the company that I travelled with.
